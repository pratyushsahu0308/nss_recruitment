National Service Scheme

National Service Scheme provides diversified opportunities to students in colleges and Universities to develop their personality through community services. National Service Scheme (NSS) is a permanent youth programme under the ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, funded by Government of Madhya Pradesh and Government of India.

Events

Induction 2023

On 18th August 2023 and 19th August 2023 Medi-Caps University conducted First Year Induction Programme. NSS Unit and Socio-Eco Club of Medi-Caps University maintained discipline and decorum throughout the Programme and helped in making it successful.

Blood Donation

NSS Unit & Socio-Eco Club of Medi-Caps University organized the 13th Blood Donation Camp on November 24, 2022, with the help of a team of doctors from MYH Hospital & in association with HDFC Bank.

NSS Day

The occasion was graced with the presence of our chief guest Shri Prakash Gadwal DAVV Program Coordinator of NSS, Honourable Vice-Chancellor Prof.(Dr) D.K. Patnaik and Honourable Pro Vice-Chancellor Prof.(Dr) D.K. Panda Volunteers actively participated in the event by performing Nukkad Natak, Dance and Singing.

Adventure Camp

Mohit Vyas volunteer of the NSS Unit, Medi-Caps University, Indore is representing Madhya Pradesh in the National level Adventure Camp of NSS which is organised at Solang, Manali, Himachal Pradesh from the 14th to the 23rd of October 2022.

Plantation Drive

NSS Unit and Socio-Eco Club of Medi-caps University has organised a "Cleanliness and Plantation Drive" on September 17, 2022. Honorable Pro-Vice Chancellor Sir, Principal of Government School Panda and Sarpanch of village Panda was there to make the drive even more blissful. With the help of many NSS and Socio-Eco Club's Volenteers the drive was successful and100+ saplings were planted.

Excursion 2022

The Socio-Eco Club of Medi-Caps University organized a 9-day excursion to Kanha Shanti Vanam, Hyderabad from 8th August 2022 to 16th August 2022. Thirty Four Volunteers along with Program Officer Dr. Nitika Vats Doohan and faculty coordinators Dr. Bhavana Shrivastava and Mr. Manish Bilgaye participated in the excursion.The excursion gave volunteers lots of memories, experiences and historic information.

Science Day

NSS Unit and Socio-Eco Club, Medi-Caps University Indore celebrated the National Science Day on 28 February 2022. The program was inaugurated by the Chancellor of the University, Mr. RC Mittal in the presence of an officer on special duty (OSD) Mr. Palash Garg and Vice-Chancellor Honorable Prof. (Dr.) D.K. Patnaik.

Vigilance Awareness

We observed the Vigilance Awareness Week Pledge, National Unity Day Pledge & Constitution Day Pledge in the presence of Honorable Pro-Vice Chancellor Dr. D.K. Panda, Registrar Mr. Ankur Saxena, Controller of Examinations Dr. Abbas Ali Koser, Special guests Manager of PNB bank Mr. Sur Singh and Head of Security Mr. Saxena Sir along with Faculty members and staff, under the guidance of Program Officer Dr. Nitika Vats Doohan Ma'am.

Convocation 2020

COVID-19 has had a profound effect on all of our lives. Social distancing and safety measures have dramatically limited and changed how we work and communicate. So through these tough times Medi-Caps University successully organised convocation for batch 2020.
